Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by web applications. A-OS uses minimal cookies for essential system functionality.

2. Cookies We Use

A-OS uses the following types of cookies:

Essential Cookies

Purpose: Authentication and session management
Duration: Session-based (cleared on logout)
Data Stored: JWT authentication tokens

Functional Cookies

Purpose: Remember user preferences
Duration: 30 days
Data Stored: Dashboard layout preferences, theme settings

3. Third-Party Cookies

A-OS does not use third-party cookies. All cookies are first-party and essential for system operation.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block all cookies (note: this will prevent system login)
  • Configure cookie preferences per-site

Note: Disabling essential cookies will prevent you from using A-OS.

5. Cookie Security

All cookies used by A-OS are:

  • HttpOnly: Not accessible via JavaScript
  • Secure: Transmitted only over HTTPS (when available)
  • SameSite: Protected against CSRF attacks

6. Updates to This Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated to reflect changes in system functionality. Check this page periodically for updates.

7. Contact

For questions about our cookie usage, contact your system administrator or the A-OS development team.
